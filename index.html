CraftersMC Guides

Farm wheat in Harvest Heaven

  • Unlock Farm Armor.
  • Grind for enchanted hay bales and sell them in the Auction House (AH).
  • Continue farming until you earn 600k–800k coins.

Next Steps:

  • Buy a Personal Compactor and set up a cobblestone farm (refer to tutorials on YouTube).
  • Grind cobblestone until you obtain a Legendary Rock Pet, then sell it to the Oliver NPC for 2 million coins.

Advance Further:

  • Build a Pumpkin Farm on your island.
  • Start grinding pumpkins while using an Epic or Legendary Bee Pet, which you can purchase from the Bee NPC.
  • Craft yourself a pair of Rancher Boots.

Congratulations! You've progressed from a beginner to a mid-game player. No more begging!

Mid-Game Guide for CraftersMc Players

Your primary focus should be unlocking minion slots, not just earning coins. The goal is to unlock 15 minion slots, which requires obtaining 200 unique minions.

  • Craft minions like Oak, Dark Oak, Spruce, Birch, Acacia, and Jungle and upgrade to Tier 9.
  • Use a Jungle Axe to speed up wood collection, contributing 45 unique unlocks.
  • Create a maxed-out Pumpkin Minion, then fund upgrades for additional slots.
  • Build Beetroot and Potato Farms to craft and upgrade their minions.
  • Visit the Fungi Cave to unlock Redstone and Lapis Minions and upgrade them.

Preparation Before Grinding Slayers:

  • Reach Farming Level 21, Foraging Level 20, and Mining Level 20.

Slayer XP Grinding:

  • Start grinding Slayer XP, following this Slayer Guide.
  • Grind until Slayer Level 4, then get Revenant Armor.
  • Farm setup and daily grind for coins and minion upgrades.
  • Upgrade gear, including a Frozen Blaze Helmet.

Minion Slots and Coin Farming:

  • Unlock up to the 19th minion slot.
  • Avoid maxing out expensive minions; stick to Tiers 7–9.
  • Setup a Magma Minion Farm to earn 800k coins per day.

Bazaar Guide for CraftersMC Players

The Bazaar is an active trading center for CraftersMC players, enabling efficient buying and selling of commodity items.

Understanding the Bazaar

  • The Bazaar has five main sections: Farming, Mining, Combat, Woods, and Miscellaneous.
  • Toggle between Direct Mode (only buy/sell prices) and Advanced Mode (detailed stats like transaction history).

Trading Options:

  • Buy Instantly: Purchase items at the lowest available price.
  • Sell Instantly: Sell items directly to the highest buy orders.
  • Create Buy Order: Set a custom quantity and price for items you wish to buy.
  • Create Sell Offer: Set a custom price for selling items from your inventory.

Strategies:

  • Flipping: Buy items at a low price and sell them at a higher price.
  • Bulk Discounts: Create large buy orders to save on costs.
  • Focus on High-Demand Items: Items like Super Compactors often have steady demand and good margins.
  • Use Presets: Save time by using preset quantities and pricing for orders.
  • At what collection will I get Legendary Rock Pet? Grind 450k+ blocks to obtain it. Sell Common, Uncommon, Rare, and Epic pets for coins.
  • What to do with Enchanted Cobblestone? Use it to craft Super Compactors to boost minion productivity.
  • What to do with coins? Save coins from Rock Pets to support future upgrades.
